About

David Dann, DMD
Dentist Located in Tarzana, CA

Dr. David Dann, DMD, has been treating patients from his office in Tarzana, California, since 1992. This father and son practice has been providing a full complement of dental services including dental implants, preventative care, and oral surgery since 1960. Dr. Dann Also provides cosmetic dentistry treatments such as teeth whitening, veneers, and the popular Invisalign® procedure. Dr. Dann's office provides sedation dentistry for a more comfortable experience.
